*TLC* is a podcast by my friends [Naomi](https://naomielenagrace.myportfolio.com), Marie, & myself. It's loosely meant to be a conspiracy theory podcast, but more times than not we get sidetracked!

We started off recording on the Wellington radio when we were studying abroad in New Zealand. After leaving New Zealand we started recording over Skype, releasing an episode about once per month. Going from a radio station to Skype has been a bit of a downgrade but it's still been a great experience!

In early 2022, I connected with [Dr. Greg Fonzo](https://dellmed.utexas.edu/directory/gregory-greg-fonzo) shortly after hearing about the recent opening of UT's *Center for Psychedelic Research & Therapy*. **Psychedelic assisted therapy** is something I believe in and I wanted to find out how I could help.

Over the course of several months I helped Dr. Fonzo in the development of the department's pilot study. My job is to build the fMRI & EEG [PsychoPy](https://www.psychopy.org/) tasks.

I've really enjoyed the work, and it makes me happy to know I contributing toward something that means a great deal to me <Emoji symbol="🙂" />.

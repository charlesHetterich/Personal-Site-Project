Diver Now is an app that I was the freelance iOS & backend developer for. Our client, Peter, wanted us to deliver the MVP of a social media app for scuba divers. Members can make posts anywhere on the world map so that other members can see pictures from their dive and then know exactly where to go if they want a similar experience!

The app was developed natively in Swift, and the backend leverages AWS services like S3 and EC2.
